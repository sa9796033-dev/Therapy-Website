About the Idea

Many patients during muscle recovery exercises often perform movements incorrectly. This leads to severe injuries, sometimes worse than the original issue. To solve this, our idea introduces a smart sensor that detects incorrect exercise movements and immediately alerts the patient with sound signals.

Project Introduction

This project aims to create a revolutionary solution in the field of physiotherapy. The sensor device will help patients during rehabilitation by detecting incorrect posture or exercise techniques. With immediate feedback, patients will avoid repeating harmful mistakes, ensuring faster and safer recovery.

Our vision: To reduce rehabilitation injuries and provide safe recovery for every patient.
